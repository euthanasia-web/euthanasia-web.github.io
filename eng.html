Hi, my name is Зачем
and I wrote a zine.

This magazine is the result of my immersion in the modern tattoo culture of Kiev, which was catalyzed by my work in the Euthanasia Sport community.

This is an emotionally centered book. Each artist is viewed primarily through the prism of my personal perception.

Graphical dialogue

I think a tattoo is more than a jewelry.
Each image is a vessel in which a person mixes his life experience and personal experiences associated with it with a similar experience and aesthetics of the artist.
The imprinting of such a bunch of meanings by traumatizing the skin is a strong emotional experience for its participants, which leads to a transformation of the personality.

In the works that are included in this zine, I explore this approach from different angles.
By modifying and complementing the imagery of other artists, I try to understand the way they think, the message and the experience they put into their work.

I see the result as a graphic dialogue of artists that are relevant to me, a symbiosis of their ideas and experiments for the sake of creating something more

You can join this dialogue if you are interested in its participants or you may not join, your life depends on you :-)
